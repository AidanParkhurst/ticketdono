For Donors

Three Simple Steps

From unused ticket to tax deduction, we handle everything in between.

01

Donate Your Tickets

Submit your unused tickets through our simple platform. We verify authenticity and assess fair market value using our IRS-compliant methodology.

02

Receive Your Statement

Get an official tax statement recognizing your charitable donation at fair market value, ready to file with your taxes.

03

Your Charity Gets Paid

Your tickets become tournament prizes. Players pay to compete in live trivia, and 100% of every entry fee goes directly to the charity you chose.

For Players

Win Real Tickets.
Support Real Causes.

Every donated ticket becomes the grand prize in a live trivia tournament open to fans everywhere. Compete head-to-head, and the fastest, most accurate player wins the tickets.

  • Pay a small entry fee to compete
  • Answer trivia about the event, team, or artist
  • Fastest + most correct player wins the tickets
  • 100% of entry fees donated to the donor's chosen charity
